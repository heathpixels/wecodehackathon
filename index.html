How To Vote

Every month, you are given 5 power tokens . These are your voting tokens. Each vote costs 1 power token . Your votes are tracked by a user name. You can change your vote as many times as you want, and allocate them to as many different topics as you want.

If you vote, and then don't show up, and you do that several times, eventually your account will be deleted. Voting closes at 5pm on Code Night to ensure that people know what topics will be covered before the event. The vote resets for the next Code Night the day after the event.
